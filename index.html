Why We Fight

Education is meant to broaden horizons, not restrict identity. The imposition of the R3 Rule on Class 9 students strips away our autonomy over what we learn and how we express ourselves.

We believe curriculum changes should empower students, not force standardizations that disregard our voices. This is a peaceful, unified call to reclaim our academic freedom. Your voice matters, and together, it is unstoppable.

Building the Coalition

As this movement scales nationally, we aim to unify our cause with larger socio-educational reforms, aligning alongside broader advocacy strategies to hold accountability at the highest levels and demand a systemic overhaul of current education policy decisions.

This is a peaceful assembly initiative organized by concerned students, parents, and educators. Know your rights. Keep it peaceful. Keep it loud.

This Protest is not directed towards any specific school or institution. It is directed towards CBSE for the new R3 rule.
